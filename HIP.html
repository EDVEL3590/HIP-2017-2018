    First Year - Mechanical Engineering

    During the first year of the program, four mentees work with different mentors to learn critical mechanical engineering aspects and ideas. I, along with the other mentees, would create robots capable of completing different tasks. We would then compete to see who had created the best design. There were many different competitions, such as a drag race, moving balls over a wall, and one of my favorites, the team competition.

    However, the program didn't only focus on engineering. The mentors also taught us how to network, create resumes, and prepare for college.
